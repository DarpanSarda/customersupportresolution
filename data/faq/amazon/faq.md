# Amazon Customer Service FAQ

## Refund Policy

### Q: What is Amazon's refund policy?
A: Amazon offers full refunds for most items returned within 30 days of delivery. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 3-5 business days after the returned item is received and inspected.

### Q: How long does an Amazon refund take?
A: Refunds typically take 3-5 business days to appear in your account after we receive and process your return. Some payment methods may take longer depending on your bank's policies.

### Q: Can I get a refund without returning the item?
A: Generally, items must be returned to receive a refund. However, for damaged or defective items, you may be eligible for a refund without return. Contact customer service for assistance.

## Shipping & Delivery

### Q: How can I track my Amazon order?
A: Track your order by going to "Your Orders" in your account, selecting the order, and clicking "Track Package". You'll also receive email updates with tracking information.

### Q: What is Amazon Prime shipping?
A: Amazon Prime members get free two-day shipping on eligible items, same-day delivery in select areas, and free release-date delivery on pre-orders.

### Q: What if my Amazon package is late?
A: If your guaranteed delivery date passes and you haven't received your package, contact Amazon customer service. You may be eligible for a free month of Prime or a refund of shipping costs.

## Returns

### Q: How do I return an item to Amazon?
A: Go to "Your Orders", find the item, select "Return or replace items", follow the prompts to print a return label, and drop off the package at the designated location.

### Q: Does Amazon have free returns?
A: Most items sold by Amazon have free returns. Some third-party sellers may charge return shipping fees. Check the return policy before purchasing.

## Prime Membership

### Q: What is Amazon Prime?
A: Amazon Prime is a membership program that offers free two-day shipping, streaming of movies and TV shows, exclusive deals, and more for $14.99/month or $139/year.

### Q: How do I cancel Amazon Prime?
A: Go to "Your Account", select "Prime Membership", click "End Membership", and follow the prompts. You'll receive a refund if you haven't used any Prime benefits since your last charge.

## Account & Security

### Q: How do I reset my Amazon password?
A: Click "Account & Lists", select "Forgot password", enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.

### Q: Is Amazon safe for online shopping?
A: Yes, Amazon uses secure payment processing and offers the A-to-z Guarantee for purchases made from third-party sellers, protecting you from fraudulent transactions.
