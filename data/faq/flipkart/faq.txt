FLIPKART CUSTOMER FAQ

REFUNDS AND RETURNS

Q: What is Flipkart's return policy?
A: Most products can be returned within 7-10 days of delivery. Some categories like electronics have different return windows. Check the product page for specific return policy details.

Q: How do I return an item on Flipkart?
A: Go to "My Orders", select the order you want to return, click "Return", choose a return reason, schedule a pickup, and pack the item. Flipkart executive will pick up the item from your doorstep.

Q: Is return pickup free on Flipkart?
A: Yes, return pickup is free for most items. In some cases, reverse pickup charges may apply - this will be clearly mentioned during the return process.

Q: How long does Flipkart refund take?
A: Refunds are initiated within 24 hours after the returned product is received. It takes 5-7 business days for the amount to reflect in your account, depending on your bank.

SHIPPING AND DELIVERY

Q: How can I track my Flipkart order?
A: Go to "My Orders", select your order, and click "Track". You'll see real-time updates on your order status. You'll also receive SMS and email updates at each stage.

Q: What are Flipkart's delivery timelines?
A: Standard delivery: 4-6 business days. Express delivery: 1-2 business days for eligible products. Same-day delivery available in select cities for specific products.

Q: What is Flipkart Plus?
A: Flipkart Plus is a free loyalty program where you earn SuperCoins on purchases. Benefits include free faster delivery, early access to sales, and exclusive offers.

PAYMENT OPTIONS

Q: What payment methods does Flipkart accept?
A: Flipkart accepts credit/debit cards, Netbanking, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), EMI options, Wallets (PhonePe, Paytm), and Cash on Delivery (eligible orders only).

Q: Is Cash on Delivery available on Flipkart?
A: Yes, Cash on Delivery is available for most orders under ₹50,000. However, some high-value items and products from certain sellers may not support COD.

Q: How does EMI work on Flipkart?
A: EMI is available on credit cards from major banks and select debit cards. No-cost EMI options are available on eligible products. Choose EMI at checkout and select your preferred tenure.

CANCELLATION

Q: How can I cancel my Flipkart order?
A: Go to "My Orders", find the order, and click "Cancel". You can cancel before the item is shipped. If already shipped, initiate a return after delivery.

Q: Will I get full refund if I cancel Flipkart order?
A: Yes, you'll receive a full refund if cancelled before shipping. Partial refunds may apply if the order has been processed or shipped.

ORDER ISSUES

Q: What if I receive wrong or damaged product?
A: Contact Flipkart customer support within 24 hours of delivery. You can request a replacement or refund through the "My Orders" section.

Q: What if my Flipkart package is stolen?
A: File a complaint within 48 hours through customer support or the app. Provide order details and any available evidence. Flipkart will investigate and may offer refund or replacement.
