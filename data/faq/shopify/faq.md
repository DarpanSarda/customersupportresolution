# Shopify Merchant FAQ

## Getting Started

### Q: How do I start selling on Shopify?
A: Sign up for a Shopify account at shopify.com, choose a pricing plan, add your products, customize your store theme, set up payment gateway, and you're ready to sell.

### Q: What do I need to start a Shopify store?
A: You need: products to sell, business information for tax purposes, a bank account for receiving payments, product images, and a domain name (optional, can buy through Shopify).

## Pricing & Fees

### Q: How much does Shopify cost?
A: Shopify offers three main plans: Basic Shopify ($29/month), Shopify ($79/month), and Advanced Shopify ($299/month). Each plan includes all core features with varying fees and transaction rates.

### Q: What are Shopify transaction fees?
A: Basic Shopify: 2.9% + 30¢ per transaction. Shopify plan: 2.6% + 30¢. Advanced Shopify: 2.4% + 30¢. Additional fees apply if you don't use Shopify Payments.

### Q: Does Shopify charge for credit card processing?
A: If using Shopify Payments: 2.9% + 30¢ (Basic), 2.7% + 30¢ (Shopify), 2.5% + 30¢ (Advanced). Third-party payment processors have their own fees.

## Products & Inventory

### Q: How many products can I sell on Shopify?
A: Shopify has no limit on the number of products you can sell. All plans support unlimited products.

### Q: Can I sell digital products on Shopify?
A: Yes, Shopify supports digital products, downloads, and digital gift cards. Use apps or built-in features for automatic digital delivery.

### Q: How do I manage inventory on Shopify?
A: Track inventory in the Products section. Enable inventory tracking to receive low stock alerts, prevent overselling, and track inventory across multiple locations.

## Payments

### Q: What payment gateways work with Shopify?
A: Shopify Payments (powered by Stripe), PayPal, Authorize.net, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and many others. Shopify Payments is available in select countries.

### Q: When does Shopify pay me?
A: Shopify Payments transfers funds to your bank account within 3 business days for most plans. payout timing varies by country and plan.

## Shipping

### Q: How do I set up shipping on Shopify?
A: Go to Settings > Shipping, create shipping zones based on location, set shipping rates (fixed, price-based, weight-based, or carrier-calculated), and add any shipping exclusions.

### Q: Can I use my own shipping carrier?
A: Yes, Shopify integrates with major carriers like USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL, and Canada Post. Buy and print shipping labels directly from your Shopify admin.

## Themes & Design

### Q: Can I customize my Shopify theme?
A: Yes, all Shopify themes are customizable. Use the theme editor to change colors, fonts, layouts, add sections, and customize your homepage and product pages.

### Q: How much do Shopify themes cost?
A: Shopify offers 12 free themes. Paid themes range from $100-$350 one-time purchase. You can also hire a developer for custom themes.

## Security

### Q: Is Shopify secure?
A: Yes, Shopify is Level 1 PCI DSS compliant, offers 256-bit SSL encryption, and includes built-in fraud protection. All transactions are secure.

### Q: Do I need SSL for my Shopify store?
A: No, all Shopify stores come with free SSL certificates. Your site automatically uses HTTPS for secure connections.
