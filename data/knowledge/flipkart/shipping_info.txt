FLIPKART SHIPPING AND DELIVERY INFORMATION
============================================

SHIPPING OPTIONS AVAILABLE
---------------------------

1. STANDARD DELIVERY (3-5 business days)
   - Available on all eligible items
   - Free for orders above ₹500
   - ₹40 shipping fee for orders below ₹500

2. EXPRESS DELIVERY (1-2 business days)
   - Available in select cities
   - Additional ₹90 fee
   - Order before 2 PM for next day delivery

3. SAME DAY DELIVERY
   - Available in Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Hyderabad
   - Orders before 10 AM delivered same evening
   - Additional ₹150 fee
   - Available on select items only

4. SCHEDULED DELIVERY
   - Choose your delivery date and time slot
   - Available in major cities
   - No additional cost
   - 2-hour delivery window

DELIVERY PIN CODE CHECK
------------------------
Before ordering, check if your pin code is serviceable:
- Enter pin code on product page
- See delivery dates and options
- Some items have restrictions on certain pin codes

TRACKING YOUR ORDER
-------------------
Track your order through:
1. Flipkart App: Orders > Track Order
2. Website: My Orders > Track Order
3. SMS/Email: Real-time updates at each stage
4. Delivery executive: Call on the day of delivery

Tracking stages:
- Order Placed
- Packing in Progress
- Shipped
- Out for Delivery
- Delivered

SHIPPING CONFIRMATION
---------------------
You'll receive:
1. Order confirmation email/SMS
2. Shipping confirmation with tracking details
3. Out for delivery notification
4. Delivered confirmation with OTP

DELIVERY INSTRUCTIONS
---------------------
Add special instructions:
- Leave at reception/security
- Call before delivery
- Neighbour delivery
- Specific delivery time

Note: These are requests and may not always be honored

FAILED DELIVERY
----------------
If delivery fails:
1. Automatic retry next day
2. Three attempts made
3. Return to sender after failed attempts
4. Refund processed automatically

AVAILABLE TIME SLOTS
--------------------
Morning: 9 AM - 12 PM
Afternoon: 12 PM - 3 PM
Evening: 3 PM - 6 PM
Late Evening: 6 PM - 9 PM

SUNDAY AND HOLIDAY DELIVERY
----------------------------
Delivery available on:
- Sundays (major cities)
- Public holidays (select locations)
- Check availability at checkout

INTERSTATE SHIPPING
-------------------
Shipping between states:
- GST may apply
- Octroi for Maharashtra (included in price)
- Entry tax for some states (included in price)
- No additional charges at delivery

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING
---------------------
Currently not available. Flipkart only delivers within India.

BULK ORDERS
----------
For bulk orders:
- Contact business@flipkart.com
- Special shipping rates
- Dedicated account manager
- Priority processing

SHIPPING PARTNERS
-----------------
Flipkart uses:
- eKart (Flipkart's own logistics)
- Blue Dart
- Delhivery
- FedEx
- India Post (remote areas)
