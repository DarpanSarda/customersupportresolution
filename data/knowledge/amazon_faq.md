# Amazon Customer Support FAQ

## Refund Policy

### How do I request a refund?
To request a refund, go to Your Orders, find the order you want to refund, and select "Request Refund." Refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days.

### What is the refund timeline?
- **Credit/Debit Cards**: 3-5 business days
- **Amazon Gift Cards**: Instant
- **Store Credit**: Instant
- **Third-party sellers**: Up to 10 business days

## Shipping

### How long does shipping take?
- **Standard Shipping**: 5-7 business days
- **Two-Day Shipping**: 2 business days
- **Same-Day Delivery**: Available in select areas
- **International Shipping**: 7-14 business days

### Can I change my shipping address?
Yes, you can change your shipping address if the order hasn't shipped yet. Go to Your Orders and select "Change Shipping Address."

## Returns

### What is Amazon's return policy?
Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Opened items may be eligible for partial refund. Some categories have different return windows:
- **Electronics**: 30 days
- **Clothing**: 30 days
- **Books**: 30 days
- **Amazon Devices**: 30 days (90 days for Amazon Prime members)

### How do I print a return label?
Go to Your Orders, find the item, and select "Return Item." You'll be able to print a prepaid shipping label.

## Prime Membership

### What are Prime benefits?
Amazon Prime includes:
- Free two-day shipping
- Same-day delivery in select areas
- Prime Video streaming
- Prime Music
- Prime Reading
- Early access to Lightning Deals

### How do I cancel Prime?
Go to Your Account > Prime Membership > End Membership. Your benefits continue until the end of the current billing period.

## Account Issues

### I forgot my password. What do I do?
Click "Forgot Password" on the sign-in page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

### How do I change my email?
Go to Your Account > Login & Security > Edit. You'll need to verify your identity before changing your email.

## Payment Issues

### Why was my payment declined?
Payment can be declined due to:
- Insufficient funds
- Incorrect billing information
- Card expired
- Bank security measures

Try another payment method or contact your bank.

### Can I use multiple payment methods?
Yes, you can split payments between multiple cards or use gift cards combined with other payment methods.
