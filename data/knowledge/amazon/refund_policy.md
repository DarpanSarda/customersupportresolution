# Amazon Refund Policy - Detailed Guide

## ELIGIBILITY FOR REFUNDS

Most items sold on Amazon are eligible for refund within 30 days of delivery. Items must be returned in original condition with all packaging and accessories.

### Return Eligibility by Category
- **Books**: 30 days
- **Electronics**: 30 days (90 days for Prime members)
- **Clothing & Accessories**: 30 days
- **Home & Kitchen**: 30 days
- **Beauty Products**: 30 days (unopened or gently used)
- **Amazon Devices**: 30 days (90 days for Prime members)
- **Third-party Sellers**: Varies by seller

## HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND

### Step-by-Step Process
1. Sign in to your Amazon account
2. Go to "Your Orders"
3. Find the order you want to refund
4. Select "Return or replace items"
5. Choose the reason for return
6. Select refund method
7. Print return label
8. Pack and ship the item

### Refund Method Options
- **Back to original payment method**: Full refund to your credit/debit card
- **Amazon Gift Card**: Instant credit to your account
- **Replacement**: Get a new item instead of refund

## REFUND PROCESSING TIME

### Amazon Items
- **Initiation**: Immediate upon return approval
- **Processing**: 1-2 business days after item received
- **Credit to account**: 3-5 business days

### Third-Party Seller Items
- **Initiation**: Seller must approve return request
- **Processing**: Up to 3 business days
- **Credit to account**: Up to 10 business days

### Amazon Gift Card Refunds
- **Processing**: Immediate upon return approval
- **Credit to account**: Instant

## RETURN SHIPPING

### Free Returns
- Amazon items: Always free return shipping
- Prime items: Always free return shipping
- Third-party: Check seller's return policy

### Return Label
- Prepaid label provided for eligible items
- Print from Your Orders page
- Drop off at any authorized location

## SPECIAL CASES

### Damaged or Defective Items
If you receive a damaged or defective item:
1. Report within 48 hours of delivery
2. Choose replacement or refund
3. No return shipping fee
4. Expedited replacement shipping

### Incorrect Item Received
If you receive the wrong item:
1. Report within 48 hours of delivery
2. We'll send the correct item
3. No need to return wrong item unless requested

### Late or Missing Refunds
If your refund is late:
1. Check Your Orders for refund status
2. Contact customer service if not processed
3. Bank processing times vary

## RESTOCKING FEES

Items with restocking fees (usually 50%):
- Opened software
- Special order items
- Custom products
- Some electronics (check product page)

## NON-REFUNDABLE ITEMS

The following cannot be refunded:
- Downloadable software products
- Gift cards (except where required by law)
- Grocery products
- Personal care products (opened)
- Custom or personalized items

## INTERNATIONAL RETURNS

For international purchases:
- Return shipping is not free
- Contact Amazon Global Customer Service
- Customs fees may apply
- Local return centers available in some countries

## AMAZON PRIME BENEFITS

Prime members get:
- Extended return window (90 days for devices)
- Free return shipping on all items
- Instant refund option (with return tracking)
- No restocking fees on eligible items
