FLIPKART RETURN AND REFUND POLICY

====================================================================

RETURN POLICY OVERVIEW
====================================================================

Flipkart offers easy returns and replacements for most products. Here's what you need to know:

RETURN WINDOW
- Electronics: 7 days from delivery
- Clothing and footwear: 10 days from delivery
- Home and kitchen: 10 days from delivery
- Books: 7 days from delivery
- Furniture: 3 days from delivery

HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN
1. Go to "My Orders" in your Flipkart app or website
2. Select the order you want to return
3. Click on "Return" or "Replace"
4. Choose a reason for the return
5. Schedule a pickup at your convenience

PICKUP PROCESS
- Flipkart will arrange for pickup from your doorstep
- Pickup is usually scheduled within 2-3 business days
- Keep the product ready with all original packaging and accessories
- The delivery executive will verify the product before pickup

REFUND TIMELINE
Once the returned product is received and verified:
- Payment via Credit/Debit Card: 5-7 business days
- Payment via Net Banking: 5-7 business days
- Payment via UPI: 3-5 business days
- Refund to Flipkart Wallet: Instant
- Refund to Gift Card: Instant

NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS
Certain items cannot be returned:
- Innerwear and lingerie
- Personal care products (once opened)
- Perfumes and deodorants
- Made-to-order products
- Customized products

EXCHANGE POLICY
For clothing and footwear, you can request a size exchange instead of a refund:
- Exchange is available once per order
- New size must be available
- Price difference will be refunded or charged accordingly

DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
If you receive a damaged or defective item:
1. Report within 24 hours of delivery
2. Share photos of the damage/defect
3. Choose between replacement or refund
4. No pickup charges for damaged/defective items

CONTACT SUPPORT
For return-related queries:
- In-app support: Available 24/7
- Customer care: 1800-202-9898
- Email: support@flipkart.com
